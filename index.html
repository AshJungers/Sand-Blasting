ABOUT US

We are a family owned, Indiana based mobile sandblasting service using crushed recycled glass. We serve customer needs in automotive, industrial, commercial, aircraft, and boat!

APPLICATIONS

Dustless Blasting is the process of using recycled bottle glass and water to remove paint without damaging sheet metal. It will leave the surface ready for primer and paint.

Soda Blasting is a revolutionary process that can remove paint, grease, and undercoating without damaging metal, glass, chrome, or warp thin sheet metal. Our process uses food grade Sodium BiCarbonate (baking soda) through our Soda Blaster by using a large volume of compressed air. It eliminates using toxis chemicals and cleans virtually any multi-layered surface.

Soda Blasting is water soluble and 100% biodegradable. So that means a safer enviorment for all. Featured on TLC, Discovery Channel, and others.

This proven technology offers superior results and can be used in wet or dry locations. So it is faster, safer, cleaner, and cost efficient. SO GO GREEN!
